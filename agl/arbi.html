This table shows how verbs change depending on how they're being used. Most of these conjugation have correpsonding pronouns. The first new column, arbizbi, moreso denotes 'someone who performs this action' usually used for things like workers, bor which the column is named. The rightmost column denotes 'a point when a verb occured'. The last two are nouns, and thus can be made into adjectives under some circumstances. Adjectives that change to the арбизби the portion before the -zbi should be referenced instead of the '-i'.

Adverbs

Adverbs are like adjectives, but for verbs. Things that will change the meaning of the verb, like appositions or adverbs, go before the verb. Adverbs will change to match the gender of the verb, which is itself inherited from the subject.
